William Carney

William Carney is an Associate Professor of English and Foreign Languages and Director of Composition at Cameron University. A New Jersey native,he directs the Internship program for his department and has published articles on pedagogy, collaboration, service learning, and community literacy. Professor Carney's research interests include ESL, technical and workplace writing, the teaching of English, and workplace and public discourse.
